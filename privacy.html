Privacy Policy

This page describes how AnJu handle user data and privacy. Effective Date: March 8, 2026

1. Introduction

AnJu ("we," "our," or "us"), founded by Juuso Anttila, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you play our mobile games, or visit our website. Our core philosophy is simple: We do not collect, store, or share your personal data.

2. Data Collection and Usage

When you play Planetary Sling, we want you to focus on the game, not on what data is being tracked. Game Progress: All of your save data, level progression, and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, nor is it transmitted to any external servers owned by AnJu. Personal Information: We do not ask for, collect, or store personal information such as your name, email address, location, or device identifiers.

3. Third-Party Services (Apple Game Center)

To provide features like achievements and cloud syncing, Planetary Sling integrates with Apple’s native Game Center services. When you log into Game Center, your gameplay achievements are securely synced with Apple's servers. AnJu does not have access to your Apple ID, password, or any personal account information associated with your Game Center profile. Data handled by Game Center is governed by Apple’s own privacy policy, which you can review at: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

4. Analytics and Tracking

We respect your digital footprint. Planetary Sling does not contain any third-party tracking software, analytics frameworks, or targeted advertising SDKs.

5. Children's Privacy

Because our game does not collect any personal data, it is completely safe for players of all ages. We comply with international children's privacy standards, including the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), as we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or any other age).

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally, especially if we introduce new features to the game. If we make significant changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at: Email: juusojanttila@gmail.com
